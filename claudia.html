The big picture.

  • I sell into 30+ convenience stores and gas stations across Austin.
  • Most of them have Hunt Brothers Pizza — customers buy pizza every day but there's nothing to dip it in.
  • We put Drip Austin right next to the pizza warmer. $2.99 impulse buy. Nobody else is doing this.
  • The demand is already there. We just need the sauce.

The idea.

  • Pizza dipping sauces made with cleaner ingredients. No seed oils — that's the only hard rule.
  • We start with a garlic butter to prove the concept. That's the flavor everybody knows.
  • But the real opportunity? Flavors nobody else is making. Italian pizza meets Mexican-inspired ingredients. Chipotle butter. Jalapeño lime. Things that make sense in Austin and the Southwest.
  • That's where your background comes in. You know these flavors better than anyone.

The requirements.

  • No seed oils. That's the one line we don't cross.
  • Shelf-stable — at least 3-6 months. This goes into stores, not a fridge.
  • Beyond that, use your creativity. You're the chef.

How we work together.

  • This is a partnership. We figure out what's fair together.
  • You bring the talent and the kitchen. I bring the stores and the brand.
  • I take care of you, you take care of me, and we build something real.

The opportunity.

There is no pizza dip category in stores.

Every sauce aisle has a hundred options for burgers and wings. Nobody has a clean dipping sauce made for pizza — let alone one with Mexican-inspired flavors. We'd be the first.

  • Hunt Brothers Pizza is in over 10,000 convenience stores across the country.
  • None of them sell a dipping sauce next to the pizza.
  • If we prove this works in Austin, the opportunity goes way beyond Austin.

Next step.

  • Start with a garlic butter. Make a test batch — something you're proud of.
  • If you have your own sauce ideas too — flavors you've been wanting to try — bring them. Your creativity is the whole point.
  • We taste it. We get feedback. We go from there.

La oportunidad.

No existe una categoría de "dip para pizza" en las tiendas.

Todos los pasillos de salsas tienen cien opciones para hamburguesas y alitas. Nadie tiene una salsa limpia hecha para pizza — mucho menos con sabores mexicanos. Seríamos los primeros.

  • Hunt Brothers Pizza está en más de 10,000 tiendas en todo el país.
  • Ninguna vende una salsa para dipear al lado de la pizza.
  • Si probamos que funciona en Austin, la oportunidad va mucho más allá de Austin.

El siguiente paso.
